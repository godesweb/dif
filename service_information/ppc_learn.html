PPC Advertising FAQs

How long until we see results from PPC?

PPC can drive immediate traffic, but typically takes 30-90 days to fully optimize for maximum performance. We usually see measurable improvements within the first 2 weeks as we refine targeting and ad copy.

What's your management fee structure?

We offer both flat-rate packages (shown above) and percentage-based plans (typically 10-20% of ad spend). For accounts spending over $20,000/month, we recommend our performance-based pricing model.

Do we need to provide creative assets?

We can work with your existing assets or create new ones. Our team includes PPC-optimized copywriters and designers who understand platform-specific best practices for ads and landing pages.
